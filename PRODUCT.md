# SureEdge — Product Context

## Register
product

## Product Purpose
SureEdge is a professional odds-trading platform for Brazilian sports bettors. It aggregates odds from 25+ bookmakers, detects surebets and duplo-green opportunities, and provides a freebet converter. Used daily by arbitrage traders who treat betting as a business.

## Users
Brazilian sports bettors / arbitrage traders. Technically savvy. Open the tool daily, often at night or during matches. Need to scan opportunities fast, act faster. Distrust anything that feels slow or amateur. Time-sensitive decisions with real money at stake.

## Brand Tone
Precise. Confident. Professional but not corporate. No filler. Every element earns its place. The tool of someone who takes this seriously.

## Anti-references
- Gambling site aesthetics (flashing banners, neon chaos, aggressive CTAs)
- Generic SaaS dashboards (cream cards, hero metrics, identical card grids)
- Crypto dark mode clichés (neon on black, aggressive glow)

## Strategic Principles
1. Speed of scanning is the core UX value — rankings, percentages, and bookmaker names must be instantly parseable
2. PA (Pagamento Antecipado) classification is a first-class signal, not a footnote
3. Score and conversion percentage are the dominant data points in every list
4. No noise — every badge, label, and color carries meaning

## Color Strategy
Restrained with committed accents: near-black surface, one green accent for profit/opportunity, one violet accent for DuploGreen data, subtle red for margin/risk badges.
